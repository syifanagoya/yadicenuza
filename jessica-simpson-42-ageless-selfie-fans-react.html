Jessica Simpson, 42, looks ageless in latest selfie

September 12, 2024
blog

The singer has previously opened up about her beauty secrets

    Jessica Simpson has rocked plenty of flawless looks on the red carpet, but she was just as picture-perfect in her latest glam selfie.

    Posing in her car, the Take My Breath Away singer, 42, wore a black fur-trimmed jacket layered over sparkly choker necklaces, while her long blonde hair cascaded over her shoulders in bouncy curls. Beauty-wise, she sported buttery skin, rosy pink blusher swept along her cheekbones and a gold and brown smokey eye with long, thick eyelashes.

    "Somedays ya just have to feel your intention and let it shine," she captioned the Instagram post, and her followers were quick to comment on her ageless appearance.

    "She’s literally ageing backwards!!" wrote one, and another penned the message: "Still looks 21, beautiful." Several others described her latest picture as "gorgeous" and "stunning."

    Jessica - who shares kids Maxwell, 10, Ace, nine, and Birdie, four, with her husband Eric Johnson - previously admitted she got into red light therapy amid the coronavirus lockdown, and she credits it for keeping her skin youthful.
