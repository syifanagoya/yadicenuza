Flipping Out Meets CSI When Jeff Helps George Eads Remodel His Home

March 02, 2024
blog

It’s Flipping Out meets CSI on the July 29 episode of Bravo’s design show when CSI star George Eads and his wife, Monika, appear. Design and real estate guru Jeff Lewis has been helping the couple renovate their home for nearly two years, and in Us Weekly‘s exclusive sneak peek at the episode, find out what happens when George finally sees his wife’s selections.

Spoiler alert: He’s a lot pickier than anyone realized. “Papa George had a very strong opinion about things,” Jenni Pulos says in an interview.

To wit: “This couch is not going to work in here,” Eads says as he tests out a bunch of sofas. “Sitting’s one thing, falling asleep on it’s another.”

Eads acknowledges that he isn’t used to working with anyone on his home decor. “I’ve never had a designer get involved,” he admits. “There’s all these little things that I didn’t know that I liked until they were gone.”
